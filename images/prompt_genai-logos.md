# GenAI Logo Prompts — doc-shape-shifter

Five text-to-image prompts for generating logo concepts. Each targets a different visual metaphor for document format transformation.

---

## 1. Origami Morphing

> Flat vector logo on a pure white background. A single sheet of paper mid-fold transforming into an origami crane. The left half of the sheet shows faint document lines and a PDF icon watermark; the right half is the crane taking shape with clean geometric facets. Use a bold two-tone color scheme of deep indigo and warm amber. Minimal detail, thick outlines, no gradients, no text. Square aspect ratio, suitable for a GitHub repo avatar.

---

## 2. Chameleon on a Document Stack

> Bold flat vector icon on a white background. A stylized chameleon perched on top of a small stack of three documents. Each document in the stack has a different colored tab (red for PDF, blue for DOCX, green for MD). The chameleon's body shifts through those same three colors in horizontal bands. Simple geometric shapes, thick black outlines, no shading or gradients, no text. Modern app-icon style, square format.

---

## 3. Rosetta Stone Keystone

> Minimalist flat vector logo on a white background. A trapezoidal stone tablet split into three vertical panels. The left panel shows a bold angle-bracket glyph representing code or markup, the center panel shows paragraph lines representing text, and the right panel shows a simple table grid. A single curved arrow arcs over the top of the stone connecting all three panels. Color palette: charcoal gray stone with teal, coral, and gold accent panels. No gradients, thick clean lines, no text. Square format.

---

## 4. Prism Refracting a Document

> Clean flat vector logo on a white background. A bold geometric triangular prism sits at center. A single white document page enters the prism from the left side. Exiting the right face of the prism are five narrow beams fanning outward, each a different saturated color (red, blue, green, purple, orange) and each ending in a tiny distinct file-type icon silhouette (page, spreadsheet grid, code brackets, book, plain rectangle). Thick outlines, no gradients, no text, modern vector art style. Square format.

---

## 5. Shape-Shifter Arrows Cycle

> Bold flat vector logo on a white background. Three rounded-corner document icons arranged in a triangular cycle. The top document is portrait-oriented with horizontal text lines, the bottom-left is a grid or spreadsheet, and the bottom-right shows angle brackets for markup. Between each pair of documents, a thick curved arrow sweeps in one direction forming a continuous clockwise cycle. Each document and its adjacent arrow share a color: electric blue, vivid green, warm orange. No gradients, minimal detail, thick strokes, no text. Square aspect ratio.
